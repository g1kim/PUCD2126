Gambas al ajillo
Gambas al ajillo

for 2 people

Tool

Frying Pan

time

20m

Kcal

654kcal

Ingredients

15 Shrimp

15 Garlic

5 Peperonchino

1/2 cup Olive oil

Little Salt

Recipe

  1. 01

    Prepare shrimp with head, tail and intestines removed, and Slices the garlic

    02

    Spread olive oil on the pot and fry garlic and pepperonchino

    03

    Add prepared shrimp and cook and finish with salt

    Tip

    It's more delicious with bread
