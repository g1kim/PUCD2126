Soufflé Omelet
Soufflé Omelet

for 1 people

Tool

Whisker(high speed of electric mixer), Frying pan

time

15m

Kcal

313.3kcal

Ingredients

3 Eggs

1 tablespoon Sugar

A little Strawberry jam

1 teaspoon butter

Recipe

  1. 01

    Beat 1 whole egg until frothy

    02

    Beat 2 eggs whites until frothy

    03

    Add 1 tablespoon of sugar and whip the meringue

    04

    Heat frying pan with low heat and put a little portion of butter

    05

    Pour in egg mixture thinly and spread the whipped meringue on the top. Then cover the lid over low heat for 5 minutes until eggs are puffed

    06

    It will going to be puffed like this after 5minutes

    07

    Then turn over and press on the side of the frying pan to make a round shape

    08

    Finally, put sugar powder and strawbarry jam

    Tip

    It is great match with rissoto or curry sauce
