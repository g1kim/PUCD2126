Fried Banana
Fried Banana

for 1 people

Tool

Frying Pan

time

20m

Kcal

300kcal

Ingredients

1 Banana

500ml Oil

50g Sugar

Recipe

  1. 01

    Cut the bananas to 2cm(1in) thickness

    02

    Fry the banana in a 180 degree temperature

    03

    Put the same amount of sugar and cooking oil in the pan

    At this time, put the sugar in the cooking oil to put the sugar does not burn quickly

    04

    Let the fire very weak and add the fried banana and syrup quickly.
