Coleslaw
Coleslaw

for 2 people

Tool

Bowl

time

10m

Kcal

301.1kcal

Ingredients

300g Cabbage

50g Onion

40g Carrots

70g Canned corn

30 grams of green pepper

4 tablespoons of mayonnaise

2 tablespoons vinegar

1 tablespoon of sugar

1/2 tablespoons salt

Recipe

  1. 01

    Chop cabbage, onions, carrots, bell peppers finely.

    02

    Put chopped vegetables into bowl and mix with mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar and salt.
